About This Website

This app is a small Star Wars-themed builder built with Node.js, Express, and JavaScript.

It works like this: you start the builder, choose one category at a time to lock in, and the rest of the categories randomize until you build a complete Force User.

After you finish, your score is shown and you can submit it to the leaderboard so you can compare your result with others.

Use the navigation bar to jump between Home, Builder, About, and Leaderboard whenever you want.
